Platform overview

Three products that run a modern IR operation.

01

Press release distribution

A newswire distribution platform integrated with Cision, Business Wire, and direct exchange feeds for same-day regulatory disclosure. Templated earnings release formats auto-populate with financial data pulled from accounting integrations, reducing the manual formatting time that delays most small-cap IR teams on reporting day. Multilingual distribution reaches international institutional shareholders with translated releases in up to twelve languages.

02

Shareholder communications

An integrated webcast and investor day platform supporting live and on-demand earnings calls, analyst days, and non-deal roadshows. Built-in Q&A moderation, live polling, and slide synchronisation eliminate the patchwork of third-party tools that most IR teams assemble for each event. Attendance data, replay metrics, and analyst engagement tracking give IR teams visibility into which institutional holders are actively engaging with management messaging.

03

Coverage and sentiment tracking

Real-time monitoring of analyst research coverage, price target changes, rating revisions, and sell-side model updates across all brokers covering the company. Shareholder register analytics track institutional ownership changes from quarterly 13F and SEDI filings, flagging significant new buyers, sellers, and positions approaching activist thresholds. Media sentiment scoring surfaces how the company is portrayed in financial press relative to sector peers.
